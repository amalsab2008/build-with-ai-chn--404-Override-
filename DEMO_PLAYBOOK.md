# SentinelAI Official Presentation Playbook 🎬

**Objective:** This document contains your exact choreography for presenting SentinelAI to stakeholders or professors. Following this script will ensure every "wow" factor hits perfectly within 3-4 minutes.

## Pre-Flight Checklist
1. Make sure your local `.env` has a valid `GEMINI_API_KEY`.
2. Open a terminal and run `npm run deploy:local` from the root directory.
3. Open a Chromium browser to `http://localhost:3000`.
4. **⚠️ CRITICAL: Turn your laptop speakers ON and turn the volume up to 100%.**
5. Fullscreen the browser so the dashboard takes up the entire screen.

---

## ⏱️ Minute 0: The Hook (Initial Startup)

**Action:** *Open the Dashboard on the projector.*
**Script:** 
> "Welcome to SentinelAI. What you are looking at is not a traditional EDR or Antivirus. It is a fully autonomous, Zero-Trust Ecosystem powered by a mixture of Generative AI experts. Usually, defense systems are reactive. I am going to show you a system that is predictive."

**Visual Cue:** Gently point to the terminal output in the bottom left corner. Note the text: `[Identity Engine] Cross-referencing Dark Web databases... Zero-Trust posture optimal.`
**Script:**
> "Before a user even logs in, our engine maps their identity against the Dark Web natively to establish baseline security."

---

## ⏱️ Minute 1: The Architecture & Topology

**Action:** *Move your mouse to the center console and click the **TOPOLOGY** button to switch from Radar to the beautiful Zero-Trust Network Graph.*
**Script:**
> "Most tools monitor a single computer. SentinelAI orchestrates the entire Enterprise mesh. Right here in the Topology view, we can see the core AI gateway communicating with end-user laptops and Kubernetes cloud nodes globally."

---

## ⏱️ Minute 2: The Red-Team War Game (The "Wow" Moment)

**Action:** *Hover your mouse over the red **INITIATE RED TEAM WAR GAME** button in the top right navigation bar.*
**Script:**
> "Relying on humans to catch threats is obsolete. Let's see how the architecture behaves under a coordinated Zero-Day attack sequence. I'm going to initiate an automated Red Team simulation."

**Action:** *Click the **INITIATE RED TEAM WAR GAME** button. **Make sure your volume is up!***
**(The system will announce: "Initiating Red Team Breach and Attack Simulation..." via audio).**

**Script:**
> "The gateway is now safely bombarding our local endpoint agent with simulated fileless injections, Mimikatz memory hooks, and ransomware payloads. Watch the dashboard react."

**Visual Cues:**
1. Let the system run. Nodes will turn Red. The AI will speak out loud across the speakers ("Critical Security Alert...").
2. The `Active Attack Chain` in the center of the screen will start flashing red.

---

## ⏱️ Minute 3: Predictive Triage (The Setup)

**Action:** *As the attack chain populates on the screen, point to the right column containing the Incident Details and the **AI Chat**.*
**Script:** 
> "The attacks are blocked, but we don't just stop there. Notice the AI Chat. We rely on a Tribunal of three AI personas—a Static Analyst, a Behavioral Analyst, and a SOC Commander—that literally debate the malware logic in real-time to prevent hallucinations."

**Action:** *Point to the blue box labeled **AI Predictive Mitre ATT&CK Forecast** inside the incident panel.*
**Script:**
> "Our AI also acts as a pre-cog. It has analyzed the malware payload and accurately predicted the next 3 probable tactics the hacker was going to try before we blocked them."

**Action:** *Click the yellow **{x} VIEW DISASSEMBLY** button.*
**(A massive Ghidra-style synthetic disassembly screen drops down).**
**Script:**
> "And for the threat intel team, the Dashboard reverse-engineers the raw Assembly bytecode instantly in the browser. We can physically see the malware attempting to call 'SetWindowsHookEx' APIs in simulated memory."

---

## ⏱️ Minute 4: Kinetic Remediation (Closing)

**Action:** *Click the **Topology** view one more time.*
**Script:**
> "Finally, blocking isn't enough; we need to isolate the patient."

**Action:** *Physically click on the Red infected Node in the Topology map.*
**Script:**
> "By clicking the infected node here, we have triggered an automated SOAR playbook that severs its digital bridge to the Core ecosystem and initiates an automated Volume-Shadow-Copy snapshot rollback. We have neutralized the threat, forecasted the attacker's motive, and healed the PC in less than a second."
